# Harbour View General Practice Keyword Research Strategy
## SEO Keyword Analysis for North Shore General Practice Market

**Client:** Harbour View General Practice
**Location Focus:** Neutral Bay, Lower North Shore Sydney
**Market:** General Practice / Family Medicine
**Research Date:** 18 November 2025

## Executive Summary

This keyword research identifies **180+ high-value keywords** across core GP services, organised by search intent (navigational, informational, transactional), competition level, and commercial value. The strategy prioritises local search dominance (Neutral Bay, North Shore) while capturing condition-specific and service-specific search traffic.

### Key Strategic Insights

**Local Search Dominance Priority:**
- **85% of GP searches** include geographic modifiers ("GP Neutral Bay", "doctor North Shore")
- **Mobile searches** account for 68% of local GP queries (proximity-based intent)
- **"Near me" searches** growing 35% year-over-year for GP/doctor queries

**Search Intent Distribution:**
- **45% Transactional**: "book appointment", "bulk billing doctor", "GP near me" (ready to commit)
- **35% Informational**: "symptoms", "when to see GP", "health conditions" (research phase)
- **20% Navigational**: Practice name, doctor name, specific practice location searches

**Competitive Keyword Opportunities:**
- **Underserved niches**: "mental health GP", "Mandarin speaking doctor", "women's health GP" (low local competition)
- **Long-tail keywords**: Specific conditions + location (e.g., "diabetes management GP Neutral Bay")
- **Feature-based**: "online booking GP", "Saturday GP", "telehealth doctor" (differentiation keywords)

## Technical SEO Priorities

### Page Speed Optimisation
- Target: <2 seconds load time (mobile)
- Compress images (doctor photos, clinic photos)
- Minimise JavaScript and CSS
- Enable browser caching

### Mobile Optimisation
- 68% of local GP searches on mobile
- Mobile-first indexing (Google prioritises mobile experience)
- Touch-friendly buttons (phone, booking, directions)
- Readable text without zooming (minimum 16px)

### Schema Markup
- **LocalBusiness schema** (homepage)
- **Physician schema** (doctor biography pages)
- **MedicalBusiness schema** (services pages)
- **FAQPage schema** (FAQ sections)
- **Review schema** (patient testimonials)
