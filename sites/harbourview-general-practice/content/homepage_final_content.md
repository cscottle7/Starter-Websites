### FAQ 1
**Q: Do you bulk bill?**

A: Yes! We bulk bill children under 16, pensioners, concession card holders, and certain eligible services including mental health care plans, GP management plans, and team care arrangements. For other consultations, we charge private fees with significant Medicare rebates available. Our fees are transparent—please see our Fees & Billing page for details or call us for specific information.

---

### FAQ 2
**Q: How do I book an appointment?**

A: You can book appointments three convenient ways:
1. **Online** (preferred): Book 24/7 through our website—instant confirmation
2. **Phone**: Call us during opening hours on (02) 9953 XXXX
3. **In person**: Visit our reception desk

Same-day appointments are often available for urgent medical concerns.

---

### FAQ 3
**Q: Can I see the same doctor each visit?**

A: Absolutely! We strongly encourage continuity of care and recommend booking with the same GP for ongoing health needs. You can select your preferred doctor when booking online or request them when calling. Seeing the same doctor allows them to understand your medical history, preferences, and health goals, leading to better personalised care.

---

### FAQ 4
**Q: Do you offer telehealth appointments?**

A: Yes! We offer telehealth consultations via video call for established patients. Telehealth is suitable for:
- Simple follow-up appointments
- Prescription renewals
- Mental health consultations
- Test result discussions
- Minor illness assessments

Your first consultation must be in-person so we can establish your medical record and get to know you.

---

### FAQ 5
**Q: What should I bring to my first appointment?**

A: Please bring:
- Medicare card (essential)
- Private health insurance details (if applicable)
- Concession card (if you have one)
- List of current medications
- Recent test results or specialist letters
- Any questions you want to discuss

Arrive 10 minutes early to complete our brief new patient registration form.
