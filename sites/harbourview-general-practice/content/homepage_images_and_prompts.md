## 9. Image Optimization Checklist

### 9.1 Pre-Upload Optimization

**All Images Must Be:**
- [ ] Properly sized for intended use (no oversized images)
- [ ] Compressed for web (<200KB for large images, <50KB for icons)
- [ ] Correct format (JPG for photos, PNG for transparency, SVG for logos/icons)
- [ ] Descriptive file names (northern-beaches-gp-consultation.jpg, not IMG_001.jpg)
- [ ] Alt text prepared for accessibility

**Tools for Optimization:**
- TinyPNG or Squoosh for compression
- SVGOMG for SVG optimization
- Photoshop or GIMP for professional editing

---

### 9.2 Accessibility Requirements

**Every Image Needs:**
- **Alt Text:** Descriptive, concise (e.g., "Female GP consulting with elderly patient in bright modern examination room")
- **Title Attribute:** Brief descriptor (optional but helpful)
- **Proper Contrast:** If text overlay, ensure 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- **Responsive Sizing:** Mobile-optimized versions where needed

## 10. Image Content Guidelines (AHPRA Compliance)

### 10.1 Medical Imagery Best Practices

**Allowed:**
- Professional medical environments
- Generic patient-doctor interactions (stock photo style)
- Medical equipment and technology
- Team and doctor portraits (with appropriate consent in real scenarios)
- Lifestyle and wellness imagery
- Educational diagrams and icons

**Avoid:**
- Identifiable real patients (unless written consent and release)
- Before/after treatment images (not applicable for GP practice)
- Graphic medical procedures
- Anything that could be perceived as guaranteed outcomes
- Overly clinical/sterile imagery that creates anxiety

---

### 10.2 Diversity and Inclusion Standards

**Representation Requirements:**
- Age diversity (children, adults, seniors)
- Gender representation (balanced, inclusive)
- Ethnic and cultural diversity (reflecting Northern Beaches community)
- Body type diversity (not only young, fit individuals)
- Family structures (diverse family compositions)
- Disability inclusion where appropriate

---

## 11. Brand Consistency Visual Guidelines

### 11.1 Photography Style Rules

**Consistent Aesthetic Across All Images:**
- **Lighting:** Bright, natural, soft (avoid harsh shadows)
- **Color Tone:** Slightly warm, inviting (not cold/clinical)
- **Saturation:** Professional but not overly vivid
- **Mood:** Optimistic, welcoming, trustworthy
- **Focus:** Sharp subjects, appropriate depth of field
- **Composition:** Professional, balanced, purposeful

---

### 11.2 Color Palette Application in Images

**Primary Colors Should Appear Naturally:**
- Blue tones (#1B75BB) in medical attire, branding elements
- Teal accents (#4DB8B8) in decor, secondary elements
- White and neutral greys in backgrounds and environments
- Natural coastal tones (ocean, sky) in lifestyle imagery
