# Blog Content Strategy - Initial Post Ideas
**Harbour View General Practice**
**Prepared:** November 2025

## Executive Summary

This document outlines three compelling, SEO-optimized blog post ideas designed to establish Harbour View General Practice's authority in the Northern Beaches community, drive organic traffic, and convert readers into patients. Each blog post idea includes a comprehensive strategy covering headline, description, target keywords, featured image concept, and detailed AI image generation prompts ready for immediate use.

### Strategic Blog Objectives
- **SEO Performance:** Target high-volume, low-competition local keywords
- **Patient Education:** Provide valuable, actionable health information
- **Trust Building:** Demonstrate medical expertise and community commitment
- **Conversion Focus:** Include clear pathways to appointment booking
- **Seasonal Relevance:** Align with current health concerns and calendar events

### 2.6 Article Structure & Key Sections

**Introduction (150 words):**
- Australia's skin cancer statistics (2 in 3 Australians by age 70)

- Northern Beaches-specific risk factors (beach culture, water sports, historical sun exposure)
- Personal risk assessment
- Family history importance

**Section 2: ABCDE of Melanoma Detection (250 words)**
- Asymmetry
- Border irregularity
- Colour variation
- Diameter (>6mm)
- Evolving (changes over time)
- Visual examples (description or infographic)
- Ugly duckling sign

**Section 3: Why Regular Skin Checks Matter (250 words)**
- Early detection saves lives
- What GP skin checks involve (full body examination, dermoscopy)
- How often to get checked (annual for most, more frequent for high-risk)
- Self-examination between checks

**Section 4: What to Expect at Your Skin Check (300 words)**
- Appointment process
- Full body examination
- Dermoscopy technology
- Biopsy if needed (explanation, not alarming)
- Follow-up care

**Section 5: Sun Protection for Northern Beaches Lifestyle (300 words)**
- Slip, Slop, Slap, Seek, Slide framework
- Beach and water sports sun safety
- Sunscreen myths debunked
- Sun protection for children
- UV index awareness

**Section 6: When to See Your GP Urgently (200 words)**
- New or changing moles
- Non-healing sores
- Suspicious lesions
- Don't delay with concerning changes

**Conclusion & CTA (150 words):**
- Early detection key message
- Encouragement to book skin check
- CTA: "Book Your Skin Cancer Screening Today"
