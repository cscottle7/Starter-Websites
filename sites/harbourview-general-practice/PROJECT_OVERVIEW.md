## Business Overview

Harbour View General Practice is a modern, patient-centred family medical practice located in the heart of Neutral Bay on Sydney's Lower North Shore. Established in 2018, the practice serves a diverse patient demographic ranging from young families to elderly residents across the North Shore suburbs.

### Billing Model

**Bulk Billing:**
- Children under 16
- Pensioners and concession card holders
- Veterans with DVA cards
- Certain eligible services (e.g., mental health care plans)

**Private Billing:**
- Standard consultations: $95 (Medicare rebate ~$41.40)
- Long consultations: $145 (Medicare rebate ~$73.25)
- Health assessments: $180-$220
