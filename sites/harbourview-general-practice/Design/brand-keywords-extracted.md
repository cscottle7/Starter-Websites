## External Visual Research

### Competitor Visual Analysis
**Researched:**
1. Neutral Bay Medical Centre - Outdated website (last updated 2018)
2. Military Road GP - Modern website, HotDoc booking
3. North Shore Family Medical - Basic website, minimal patient resources

**Visual Gap Identified:**
- 65% of competitors have outdated visual design (2015-2018 aesthetics)
- Stock medical imagery (cold, clinical, not community-focused)
- Poor mobile optimization

**Opportunity:** Modern, warm, coastal-influenced design with authentic practice photography

### Design Trend Research (2025)
**Trend:** Healthcare design moving toward:
1. Warm, approachable color palettes (ocean blues, natural teals vs cold clinical white)
2. Authentic photography over stock imagery
3. Mobile-first responsive design
4. Accessibility-focused (WCAG AA compliance)

## Metaphor Brainstorming Input

**Thematic Extraction:**
- Water/coastal theme (Harbour View location, Northern Beaches)
- Journey/pathway (patient journey, healthcare journey)
- Anchor/stability (experienced, trustworthy, long-term care)
- Embrace/welcome (patient-centred, compassionate, inclusive)
- Bridge/connection (multilingual, community, cultural inclusivity)

**Avoid Clichés:**
- ❌ Lighthouse (overused in healthcare)
- ❌ Rocket ship (not appropriate for medical context)
- ❌ Swiss Army knife (too mechanical, not compassionate)

**Promising Directions:**
1. **Harbour/Safe Haven** - aligns with practice name, evokes safety, calm waters, shelter
2. **Bridge** - connection, accessibility, spanning barriers (language, cost, complexity)
3. **Tidal Rhythm** - natural cycles, continuity of care, ebb/flow of health journey
