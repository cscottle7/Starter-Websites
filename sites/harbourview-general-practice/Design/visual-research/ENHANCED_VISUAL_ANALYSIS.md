- Deep blues (#1B75BB, #0066CC)
- Medical whites (#FFFFFF, #F8F9FA)

- Minimal accent colors

**Layout Patterns:**
- Symmetric 50/50 grids
- Uniform card heights (items-stretch)
- Centered hero sections
- Traditional medical hierarchy

**Typography Trends:**
- Sans-serif body (Inter, Roboto, Open Sans)

- Professional, corporate feel

**Mood:** Trust, professionalism, clinical competence

## Layout Strategy (Magazine Editorial)

**Full-Bleed Hero:**
- Edge-to-edge imagery
- Overlapping text/image layers
- Diagonal clip-path sections
- Dynamic, not static

**Unexpected Grid Breaks:**
- Services section: Offset masonry grid
- Doctor profiles: Overlapping card stacks
- Testimonials: Pull quotes breaking container bounds

**Creative Hierarchy:**
- Stats LARGER than headings (e.g., "350 patients" bigger than "Mental Health")
- Visual reversal (small text intro, HUGE impact stat)

---

## Typography Direction (Display + Inversion)

**Headings: Display Font** (Editorial feel)
- Recommendation: Playfair Display (serif), or
- Syne (geometric display), or
- Cabinet Grotesk (modern editorial)
- Bold, confident, distinctive

**Body: Humanistic Sans**
- Inter or DM Sans (readable, modern)
- Contrast with display headings

**Hierarchy Inversions:**
- Patient testimonial quotes: text-5xl
- Section headings: text-3xl
- Subheadings: text-xl
- (Reverses traditional hierarchy)
