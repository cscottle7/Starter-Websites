**Specialization:** Ear, Nose, and Throat (ENT) Medicine
**Primary Services:**
- Rhinitis Treatment (Allergic and Non-Allergic)
- Sleep Apnea Solutions (Obstructive Sleep Apnoea)
- Deviated Nasal Septum Care
- General ENT Consultations

**Primary Archetype:** The Caregiver
**Secondary Archetype:** The Sage

**Brand Values:**
- **Compassionate Care:** Patient comfort and wellbeing are paramount
- **Clinical Excellence:** Evidence-based treatment with latest medical advances

## Tone of Voice

**Primary Tone:** Professional yet Approachable

**Voice Characteristics:**
- **Clear:** Medical terminology explained in plain language
- **Reassuring:** Calm, confident, trustworthy
- **Educational:** Informative without being condescending
- **Empathetic:** Understanding patient concerns and anxieties
- **Direct:** Straightforward about treatments and outcomes

**Writing Guidelines:**
- Use second person ("you") to create personal connection
- Explain medical terms when first introduced
- Focus on patient benefits and outcomes
- Avoid medical jargon unless necessary (then explain)
- Use active voice for clarity
- Keep sentences concise and scannable

**Example Phrases:**
- ✅ "We'll help you breathe easier with personalized rhinitis treatment"
- ✅ "Understanding your sleep apnea symptoms is the first step to better rest"
- ❌ "Utilization of advanced rhinologic therapeutic interventions"
- ❌ "OSA management through multimodal approaches"

## Accessibility Requirements

All content must meet **WCAG 2.1 AA** standards:
- Color contrast ratios: Minimum 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text
- All images include descriptive alt text
- Forms have clear labels and error messages
- Keyboard navigation functional throughout
- Screen reader compatibility verified

## Compliance & Legal

**Medical Disclaimers:**
- All blog posts include standard medical disclaimer
- Treatment descriptions note "results may vary"
- Privacy policy clearly visible (footer link)
- HIPAA compliance for any patient testimonials

**Required Footer Elements:**
- Copyright notice
- Privacy Policy link
- Terms of Use link
- Professional credentials/licensing information
