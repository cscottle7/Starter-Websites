## Business Overview

Sydney Dermatology Specialists is a premium specialist dermatology practice providing comprehensive medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology services across two convenient Sydney locations. Established in 2016, the practice is led by two experienced specialist dermatologists dedicated to evidence-based, patient-centred care for all skin, hair, and nail conditions.

### Practice Philosophy

"Expert dermatology care combining clinical excellence with compassionate, personalised service. We treat every patient as an individual, taking time to understand your concerns and develop tailored treatment plans using the latest evidence-based approaches."

### Services Offered

**Medical Dermatology**
- Comprehensive skin condition diagnosis and management
- Psoriasis treatment (topical, systemic, biologics)
- Eczema and atopic dermatitis management
- Acne treatment (topical, oral, isotretinoin supervision)
- Rosacea management
- Autoimmune skin conditions (lupus, dermatomyositis, bullous diseases)
- Hair loss and alopecia diagnosis and treatment
- Nail disorders
- Infectious skin conditions
- Drug reactions and allergic skin conditions
- Paediatric dermatology (children and adolescents)

**Surgical Dermatology & Skin Cancer**
- Skin cancer screening and full body skin checks
- Dermoscopy and digital dermoscopy monitoring
- Skin cancer excision and repair
- **Mohs micrographic surgery** (Dr. Harper - highest cure rate for certain skin cancers)
- Flap and graft reconstruction
- Benign lesion removal (moles, cysts, lipomas, skin tags)
- Scar revision

**Cosmetic Dermatology**
- Anti-wrinkle injections (botulinum toxin - frown lines, crow's feet, forehead lines)
- Dermal fillers (volume restoration, facial contouring, lip enhancement)
- Laser skin resurfacing (CO2 fractional laser, Fraxel)
- Laser hair removal
- Laser treatment for vascular lesions, pigmentation, redness
- Chemical peels (glycolic acid, TCA, VI Peel)
- Microneedling with radiofrequency (Morpheus8, Fractora)
- IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) photorejuvenation
- Treatment of melasma, sun damage, age spots
- Acne scarring treatment

**Diagnostic Services**
- Digital dermoscopy with photography and mapping
- Skin biopsy (punch biopsy, shave biopsy, excisional biopsy)
- Patch testing for contact dermatitis/allergies
- Skin imaging and mole monitoring

## Competitive Positioning

### Market Position

**Premium Specialist Practice**
- Positioned as high-quality, evidence-based specialist dermatology
- Not luxury/boutique tier (not $600+ consultations)
- Not budget/bulk-billing tier
- Mid-to-premium pricing with strong clinical credentials

### Key Differentiators

**1. Dual-Specialist Model**
- Dr. Harper: Medical/surgical dermatology, Mohs surgery expertise
- Dr. Nguyen: Cosmetic dermatology, acne/pigmentation specialist
- Broad service coverage under one roof

**2. Mohs Micrographic Surgery**
- Only **6 Mohs surgeons in Sydney** (highly specialised)
- Highest cure rate for basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma
- Tissue-sparing technique (better cosmetic outcomes)
- Competitive advantage for complex skin cancer cases

**3. Two Convenient Locations**
- Sydney CBD (accessible for city workers, public transport hub)
- Chatswood (North Shore families, parking availability)
- Flexible appointment locations

**4. Evidence-Based, Patient-Centred Care**
- Detailed consultations (30-45 minutes for new patients)
- Clear treatment explanations, shared decision-making
- No rushed "cosmetic factory" approach

**5. Comprehensive Service Range**
- Medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology
- Paediatric to geriatric patients
- Minimises need for multiple specialist referrals
