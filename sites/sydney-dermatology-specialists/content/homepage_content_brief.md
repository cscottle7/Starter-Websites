### Section 4: Mohs Surgery Excellence (Differentiator Highlight)

**Heading:** "Mohs Micrographic Surgery: The Gold Standard for Facial Skin Cancer"

**Content:**

Dr. Alexandra Harper is one of only **six Mohs surgeons in Sydney**, offering the highest cure rate (98-99%) for basal cell carcinoma and squamous cell carcinoma, particularly in cosmetically sensitive areas like the face.

Mohs surgery is a tissue-sparing technique that removes skin cancer layer by layer while preserving maximum healthy tissue—resulting in smaller scars and superior cosmetic outcomes compared to traditional excision.

**When is Mohs surgery recommended?**
- Skin cancers on the face, ears, nose, eyelids
- Large or aggressive skin cancers
- Recurrent skin cancers
- Skin cancers with poorly-defined borders

### Section 9: FAQ

**Q: Do I need a GP referral to see a dermatologist?**
A: GP referrals are not required but are recommended. Referrals ensure Medicare rebates for specialist consultations and allow us to review your medical history and GP's clinical findings before your appointment.

**Q: How much do dermatology consultations cost?**
A: Initial specialist consultations are $350 (Medicare rebate $158.95, out-of-pocket ~$191). Cosmetic consultations and treatments are not covered by Medicare. View our full fee schedule.

**Q: What should I bring to my first appointment?**
A: Bring your GP referral letter (if applicable), Medicare card, private health insurance details, list of current medications, and any previous test results or dermatology reports.

**Q: How long will I wait for an appointment?**
A: We aim to see new patients within 2-4 weeks for routine appointments. Urgent skin cancer cases are prioritised and seen within 1 week.
