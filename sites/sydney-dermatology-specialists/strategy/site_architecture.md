# Website Site Architecture
**Sydney Dermatology Specialists**
**Prepared:** November 2025

## Executive Summary

This site architecture document defines the complete information architecture, URL structure, navigation hierarchy, and internal linking strategy for Sydney Dermatology Specialists. The architecture establishes the practice as Sydney's premier comprehensive dermatology destination, with strategic emphasis on rare Mohs micrographic surgery capability, integrated medical-surgical-cosmetic services, and evidence-based patient care.

### Architecture Overview
- **Total Pages:** 33 pages
- **Primary Navigation Items:** 6
- **Service Hub Pages:** 3 major hubs (Skin Cancer, Medical Dermatology, Cosmetic Dermatology)
- **Individual Service Pages:** 15
- **Supporting Content Pages:** 9
- **Functional Pages:** 6
- **URL Structure:** Clean, keyword-optimized, specialist-focused hierarchy
- **Mobile-First Design:** Premium user experience across all devices
