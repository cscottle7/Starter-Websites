## Cross-Journey Insights: Specialist Dermatology Unique Factors

### What Differentiates Specialist Dermatology Journeys

#### 1. Specialist Credentials Critical
- **FACD qualification** is non-negotiable trust signal
- Patients research: "What is FACD?" to understand difference between specialist dermatologist, cosmetic doctor, GP
- Fellowship training (e.g., Mohs surgery) is powerful differentiator

#### 2. Medical vs Cosmetic Patient Expectations
- **Medical Dermatology Patients:** Seeking cure, relief, expertise for complex condition
- **Cosmetic Patients:** Seeking natural enhancement, medical safety, evidence-based approach (not cosmetic clinic "sales" environment)

#### 3. Referring GP Relationship Essential
- 75% of specialist dermatology patients are GP-referred
- GP trust and satisfaction with specialist communication drives ongoing referrals
- Specialist must balance: excellent patient care + maintaining GP relationship

#### 4. Treatment Outcomes Define Loyalty
- Dermatology is results-driven specialty
- Successful outcomes (skin cancer cure, acne clearance, psoriasis control, natural cosmetic enhancement) create fiercely loyal advocates
- Poor outcomes (recurrent skin cancer, cosmetic complications) result in negative reviews and referral loss
