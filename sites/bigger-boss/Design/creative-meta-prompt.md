## Section 4: Creative Puzzle - Design Challenge

### Design Challenge Statement

**Challenge:**
"Design a homepage that visually demonstrates professional B2B credibility (for risk-averse clients requiring AHPRA/ASIC compliance) WHILE achieving 60-70% differentiation from generic SaaS competitors (Stripe, Vercel, Linear) through bold visual choices that DON'T sacrifice trustworthiness."

### Creative Constraints (5 Anti-AI Design Rules)

1. **Spatial Rhythm (NOT Uniform):**
   - Challenge: Prevent default 4rem uniform padding
   - Solution: Alternate 5rem and 6rem section padding (engineered breathing room)
   - Verification: Section padding varies between 5-6rem (NOT uniform)

2. **Intentional Asymmetry (NOT Perfect Grid):**
   - Challenge: Prevent AI-default perfect grid alignment
   - Solution: Alternate icon positions in card grids (left, center, right, left pattern)
   - Verification: Differentiator cards have non-uniform icon placement

3. **Unexpected Hierarchy (NOT CTA-First):**
   - Challenge: Prevent generic "CTA above fold" default
   - Solution: Metrics row BEFORE primary CTA (data-first credibility)
   - Verification: Hero layout: Headline â†’ Metrics â†’ CTA (NOT Headline â†’ CTA)

4. **Color Nuance (NOT Electric Accent):**
   - Challenge: Prevent electric orange #FF6B35 (too playful for Professional B2B)
   - Solution: Muted Orange #D97642 (controlled warmth, WCAG AA compliant)
   - Verification: Orange contrast 4.1:1 on white (professional, NOT aggressive)

5. **Constraint-Driven Geometry (NOT Default Radius):**
   - Challenge: Prevent default 0.5rem border-radius (generic) or sharp 0.25rem (harsh)
   - Solution: Progressive radius: Buttons 0.375rem, Cards 0.5rem, Containers 0.75rem
   - Verification: Border-radius intentionally varied (machined precision)
